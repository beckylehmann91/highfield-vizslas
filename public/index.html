About Highfield

At Highfield, we've been breeding Vizslas for nearly two decades. Breeding only quality dogs ensures the integrity of this wonderful breed. These dogs exude joy, have very affectionate dispositions, and love their families (especially the children). Their sense of smell is exceptional, making them outstanding field dogs. Whether you hunt, jog, or just walk, they love being at your side!

To learn more about the breed, I recommend Versatile Vizsla, by Marion L. Coffman, it's an excellent book.
